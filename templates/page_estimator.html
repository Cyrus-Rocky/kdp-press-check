How trim size affects page count

  • 5×8 (small paperback): more pages, more expensive to print
  • 6×9 (standard trade paperback): the most common KDP size
  • 8.5×11 (large format): fewer pages, lower print cost
  • A 70,000-word novel can range from 220 pages (8.5×11) to 380 pages (5×8)
