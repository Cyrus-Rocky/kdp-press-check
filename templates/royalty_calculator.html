How KDP royalties work

  • Paperback: You earn 60% of your list price, minus the printing cost. KDP pays the difference.
  • Kindle eBook: 70% royalty for books priced $2.99–$9.99, minus a small delivery fee. 35% for all other prices.
  • Minimum price: You must price your book high enough that your royalty is at least $0.00 after printing costs.
  • Rates shown are for the US marketplace. Other markets vary slightly.

Printing cost formula (US)

  • B&W: $0.85 fixed + $0.012 per page
  • Premium Color: $0.85 fixed + $0.07 per page
  • Example: 300-page B&W book = $0.85 + (300 × $0.012) = $4.45
