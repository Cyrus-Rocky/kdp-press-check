What this does

Renders your actual PDF pages at screen resolution so you can catch layout surprises — text running into the gutter, chapter headings that land on a wrong page, images that look off — before KDP ever sees the file. The first {{ max_pages }} pages are shown (cover + up to {{ max_pages - 1 }} interior pages).

  • Navigate with arrow keys or the Prev / Next buttons
  • Two-page spread view mirrors how a reader holds the book
  • Cover shown as a standalone front-cover display
  • Nothing is stored — files are deleted immediately after rendering
